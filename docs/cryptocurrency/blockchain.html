Overview

Web3

Web3 is a decentralized version of the internet that empowers users with privacy and enables digital transactions through blockchain-based applications.

Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrencies (like Bitcoin and Ethereum) are fungible digital assets that can be exchanged for one another as each cryptocurrency can be traded for another of the same value.

NFT (Non-Fungible Token)

NFTs are unique blockchain-based digital assets that prove ownership and authenticity of items like art, music, videos, virtual goods or real estate. Unlike cryptocurrencies, which are interchangeable, NFTs are distinct and cannot be exchanged on a one-to-one basis.

Pump Fun

Pump Fun is a Solana-based platform that allows users to create and trade meme coins (tokens) and launch them onto decentralized exchanges (DEXs).

Hyperledger Fabric

Hyperledger Fabric offers a number of SDKs to support developing blockchain applications (smart contracts, chaincode) in various programming languages (Node.js, Java, Go).
