Strategies

Buy Low, Sell High

Futures

A derivative contract to buy or sell an asset at a predetermined price on a future date.

HODL

Buy and hold assets for a long period, regardless of volatility (fluctuations), expecting long-term gains.

Scalping

Make small profits quickly and repeatedly by taking advantage of minor price movements.

Arbitrage

Capitalize on price differences between different markets or exchanges by buying and selling the same asset.
