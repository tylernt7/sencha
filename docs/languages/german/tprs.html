TPRS Mini Story (Teaching Proficiency through Reading and Storytelling)

Schneckenwitz

If you practice with the Question and Answer technique and with different stories regularly, you will soon start to think in German. Only if you are able to think in German, you will be able to speak it fluently.

Repetition is the key factor in the learning process! Even the most difficult lesson and story will become easier after a few repetitions.

Ein Mann steht auf seinem Balkon.
Wer steht auf seinem Balkon? Ein Mann.
Wo steht der Mann? Auf seinem Balkon.
