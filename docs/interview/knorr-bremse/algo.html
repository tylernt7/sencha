Algorithm

(Attila Madaras)

- You have a list of integers. Figure out an algorithm that returns whether or not 3 elements in the list add up to 0.
- Adott integerek listája. Találd meg, hogy van-e benne olyan 7 hosszú részsorozat, amelyben 0-tól 6-ig minden szám szerepel!
- You have 8 balls. One of them is defective and weighs less than others. You have a balance to measure balls against each other. In 2 weightings how do you find the defective one?

3 steps: binary

I assume that the balls are numbered.

step 1) choose ball 1 and 2 and put them aside
weight balls 3 4 5 against 6 7 8
if equal, then check 1 against 2, and we are done.

step 2) let's say that 3 4 5 are found lighter.
we now know that 6 7 and 8 are normal.

put 3 aside
put 4 with 6
put 5 with 7
weight 4 6 against 5 7.
If equal then it is ball 3,
if 4 6 is lighter then it is ball 4
if 5 7 is lighter then it is ball 5.

- Írj egy metódust, ami megmondja egy számról, hogy elölről hátra olvasva ugyan az-e az értéke, mint hátulról előre olvasva!
