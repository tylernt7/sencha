Hand Reflexology

- Press with thumb for 5 seconds & release for 3 seconds, in the affected point. Repeat for 2-3 minutes for 5 to 10 days.

- Apply gentle pressure for 3-5 seconds in a circular motion in clockwise direction and then in counter-clockwise direction.

- Hand reflexology works on opposites. This means the reflex points on the left hand correspond to the body parts on the right side, and the right reflex points correspond to the body parts on the left.
