Breathing Exercises

1. Pursed lip breathing

Inhale (nose) ==> Exhale (pursed lips)

2. Coordinated breathing

Inhale (nose): lower the weight ==> Exhale (pursed lips): lift the weight

3. Deep breathing

Inhale (nose) ==> Hold your breath ==> Exhale (nose)

4. Huff cough

Inhale (mouth) ==> Hold your breath ==> Exhale (mouth) in 3 breaths

5. Diaphragmatic breathing

Inhale (nose) ==> Exhale (pursed lips)

4-7-8 technique

Inhale (nose) 4 sec ==> Hold your breath 7 sec ==> Exhale (pursed lips) 8 sec
